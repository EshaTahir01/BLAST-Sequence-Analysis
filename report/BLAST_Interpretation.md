# BLAST Result Interpretation

## Objective

To compare the hemoglobin beta protein sequences of human and mouse using NCBI BLAST.

## Interpretation of Results

### Alignment Score

The alignment score was approximately 260 bits, indicating a strong alignment between the two protein sequences.

### Percent Identity

The sequences shared a high percentage of identical amino acids, demonstrating that they are closely related.

### Similarity (Positives)

Approximately 90% similarity was observed, showing that many amino acids have similar biochemical properties even when they are not identical.

### Gaps

Only a few gaps were present, suggesting minimal insertions or deletions during evolution.

### E-value

An E-value of 0.0 indicates that the alignment is highly significant and is extremely unlikely to have occurred by chance.

## Conclusion

The BLAST analysis demonstrates that human and mouse hemoglobin beta proteins are evolutionarily conserved and perform similar biological functions.
