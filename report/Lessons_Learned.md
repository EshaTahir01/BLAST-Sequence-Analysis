# Lessons Learned

## Project

BLAST Sequence Analysis

## What I Learned

- I learned how to use the NCBI BLAST tool for sequence comparison.
- I learned how to perform pairwise sequence alignment between protein sequences.
- I understood the meaning of alignment score, E-value, percent identity, and similarity.
- I observed that human and mouse hemoglobin beta proteins are highly similar, indicating evolutionary conservation.
- I improved my ability to interpret BLAST results and biological sequence data.

## Challenges

- Understanding the different BLAST output parameters initially required careful reading.
- Learning how to interpret biological significance rather than just numerical values.

## Future Improvements

- Explore different BLAST programs such as BLASTn and BLASTp.
- Analyze additional genes and proteins from different organisms.
- Apply BLAST results in comparative genomics and evolutionary studies.
